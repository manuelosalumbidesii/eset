Our Services
Solar Panel Installer: This is a hands-on role where you'll be installing solar panels on rooftops and ground structures. You'll also be responsible for wiring the system and ensuring everything is safe and functional.
Solar Sales Representative: If you're more people-oriented, this role might be a good fit. You'll educate potential customers about the benefits of solar power, design systems for their needs, and close deals.
Solar System Designer: This technical role involves using software to design and engineer solar photovoltaic (PV) systems. You'll need a strong understanding of solar power technology, building codes, and electrical engineering.
Solar Project Manager: As the project manager, you'll oversee the entire solar installation process, from initial sales to system activation. You'll manage budgets, timelines, and ensure the project meets all safety and quality standards.
Solar Service Technician: This role involves troubleshooting and maintaining existing solar power systems. You'll diagnose problems, perform repairs, and ensure the system is operating efficiently.
